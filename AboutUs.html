About our Head Baker

At Caca Milis we strive to deliver the best experience
possible to our customers from ordering, designining or
sampling the
very best tasting work of art possible.

Sophie, owner and head baker here at Caca Milis has a B.Sc
in Baking and Pastry Arts Management which she studied in
Dublin Cookery School. She is constantly browsing websites
and reading magazines in an effort to refine her recipes
and combine her techniques with innovative modern ingredients.

Caca Milis is registered with the Environmental Health Executive
of Ireland and is HACCP certified.
